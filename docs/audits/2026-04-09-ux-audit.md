# EPB Site — UI/UX Audit

**Date:** 2026-04-09
**Scope:** `https://epb-v1.vercel.app/` (live) + source in `workspaces/epb/src/`
**Method:** Playwright viewport capture (1440×900 desktop, 390×844 mobile/iPhone 14), DOM measurement, a11y scan, source cross-reference against the `ui-ux-pro-max` rulebook.
**Overall verdict:** The home page looks strong at the **hero level** — solid typography, clear brand voice, good color restraint. Below the fold the site **bloats dramatically**, the mobile navigation pattern is broken, and the contact flow has no form. The biggest problems are structural, not cosmetic.

---

## Top 10 issues (priority order)

| # | Severity | Area | Issue |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Critical | Mobile nav | Horizontal-scroll chip bar hides 2 of 5 items off-screen (259 px clipped); chips are 34 px tall (below 44 px WCAG min) |
| 2 | Critical | Contact | Contact section has no form. It's 4 link-cards. For a B2B firm whose CTA is "consultá", this is the #1 conversion blocker |
| 3 | Critical | Press | 5 press cards show "Contenido pendiente" live in production (`PressSection.tsx:28`) |
| 4 | High | Header | Two elements labeled "Contacto" with different destinations (anchor vs WhatsApp) — classic navigation anti-pattern |
| 5 | High | Services | "Servicios" section contains only 1 real service card + 1 promo for AgenciaPalmero. Section title is plural but content is singular |
| 6 | High | Recognitions | Three massively unbalanced columns (6/7/20+ items). Santander alone runs ~20 rows while other columns sit in whitespace. Adds ~1500 px of scroll |
| 7 | High | Mobile length | Mobile page is 19,571 px tall (~23 viewport heights). Primary bloat: Recognitions + Press stacking in one column |
| 8 | High | Clients | Client logos at `opacity:0.6 + grayscale(1)` → ~2.3:1 effective contrast, failing WCAG 1.4.11 (3:1 for UI elements) |
| 9 | Medium | `og:image` | Meta OG image points to `epb.vercel.app` but the live site is `epb-v1.vercel.app` → broken social preview |
| 10 | Medium | Motion contract | `.reveal` starts at `opacity:0` and only unhides on `IntersectionObserver`. Print / screen-reader-heavy / crawler / failed-JS states can render blank |

## Desktop findings (1440 × 900)

### Hero — Strong
- Clear H1, good rhythm, bilingual-safe typographic scale, stats card is well-composed.
- CTAs are ≥44 px, well-spaced, correct hierarchy (primary white pill + ghost outline).
- Nit: 5-line title stack is aggressive; widen title container so it breaks in 3 lines.

### Clients
- Logo carousel is too faint (opacity 0.6 + grayscale). For an institutional credibility wall (Galicia, Santander, Hipotecario) these are the #1 trust signal and should pop.
- Stats chips duplicate hero stats content — same numbers, two places.
- Recommendation: opacity 0.85, drop grayscale, `hover:opacity-100`, centralize stats in hero only.

### Services
- Title "SERVICIOS" (plural) renders as a single card.
- Second panel `<aside>` promoting AgenciaPalmero has equal visual weight to primary service.
- Missing taxonomy: cobranzas, recupero, gestión de mora (temprana + tardía), B2B/B2C.
- Recommendation: 3-card grid (Gestión de mora / Recupero de activos / Gestión de cobranzas). Demote AgenciaPalmero to a "Marcas del grupo" strip.

### Herramientas
- 5 text-only cards, no icons, no visual anchors.
- Cards use `• text` bullet prefixes inside `<li>` — textual dots double up with list semantics.
- Unequal card heights.
- Recommendation: add Lucide icons (Database, LineChart, Phone, Handshake, ShieldCheck), normalize heights, lift keyword chips out of prose.

### Nosotros
- Left card has no heading — asymmetric vs right column.
- No human signal — no founder, no team, no photo.
- Valores chips look like tag filters — unclear affordance.
- Recommendation: add small leadership block with photo + name + role.

### Reconocimientos
- Three unbalanced columns with huge whitespace under short ones.
- 20+ awards reads as fatigue, not prestige.
- No temporal grouping, icons, or tier indication.
- Recommendation: masonry OR "Show more" collapse after 6 items per client OR horizontal timeline. At minimum, summary chip ("+30 reconocimientos · 2009–2019") with expandable detail.

### Prensa
- 5 cards show "Contenido pendiente" in production.
- Redundant "PRENSA" label on every card.
- No dates, no thumbnails.
- Recommendation: hide cards without URLs OR wire real URLs. Add dates + optional thumbnail.

### Contacto — Critical
- No form. Four CTA cards only.
- Phone appears twice (sidebar + card).
- Redundant labeling "Quiero comunicarme / Contacto comercial".
- "Portal de autogestión" fights with commercial CTA (two different audiences).
- Horario de atención as long prose lines — hard to scan.
- Recommendation: add 4-field inline form (Nombre, Empresa, Email, Mensaje). Split Portal de autogestión into its own small strip for debtor self-service.

### Footer — OK
- Clean column layout.

## Mobile findings (390 × 844, iPhone 14)

### Nav is broken
- Horizontal-scroll chip bar: scrollWidth 649 px in clientWidth 390 px container. Novedades + Contacto off-screen, require swipe inside a narrow lane.
- Chip touch targets 34 px tall (iOS min 44 pt).
- Header + chip bar = 127 px (~15% of viewport) permanently fixed.
- No hamburger / drawer.
- Recommendation: Replace chip bar with hamburger → full-screen drawer. Hamburger ≥44×44, drawer items ≥48 px tall, active section indicator via scrollspy.

### Hero
- Content stacks well, CTAs full-width, spacing solid.
- Stats card pushed ~700 px below the fold; each stat stacks vertically.
- Recommendation: render stats as compact 3-across inside the hero, OR drop them on mobile.

### Clients
- Faint logos wash out on cream background.
- "TRAYECTORIA" label clips on leftmost stat chip.

### Services / Herramientas / Nosotros / Reconocimientos
- Everything stacks to single column.
- Recognimientos becomes endless scroll of identical bullet lines.

### Contact
- All 4 CTA cards stack. Reinforces finding #2.

### Page length
- 19,571 px mobile vs ~9,919 px desktop (~1.9×). Recognitions + Press multi-column on desktop collapse without density adjustment.

## Code-level bugs to fix

1. **`PressSection.tsx:28`** — placeholder `"Contenido pendiente"` leaks when press items have no URL.
2. **`ContactSection.tsx:45-52`** — `Teléfono` card duplicates `CONTACT.phone` already shown in sidebar.
3. **`Header.tsx:28-36`** — `Contacto` CTA shares label with nav link but targets WhatsApp with `target="_blank"`. Add `aria-label` or rename.
4. **`Header.tsx:39-52`** — mobile chip nav should be replaced with `<button aria-expanded>` hamburger + drawer.
5. **`RecognitionsSection.tsx:21`** — remove hard-coded `"• "` prefix; restore CSS `::marker`.
6. **`App.tsx:14` / `useScrollReveal.ts`** — starting opacity 0 risks blank content in crawlers/print/failed-JS. Guard with a class on `<html>` set after JS confirms support, OR use `content-visibility: auto`.
7. **`index.html` OG tags** — fix `epb.vercel.app` → `epb-v1.vercel.app` (or canonical production domain) in `og:image`.

## Factory gaps — skills & agents missing for web UX audits at scale

### Skills used
- `ui-ux-pro-max`, `a11y-audit`, `design`, `ui-styling`, `site-architecture`, `motion`.

### Misaligned
- `ui-ux-pro-max` is explicitly scoped to **App UI (iOS/Android/RN/Flutter)** — its checklist (safe areas, Dynamic Type, haptics, system gestures) doesn't quite fit web. No web-specific equivalent.

### Missing skills
1. **`web-ui-audit` / `responsive-audit`** — web audit skill covering desktop breakpoints (1024/1280/1440/1920), container queries, tablet landscape, print, `@media hover:none`, CLS budget, LCP budget, font swap behavior, scroll-reveal pitfalls.
2. **`conversion-audit` / `landing-audit`** — B2B funnel friction (form length, CTA clarity, trust signals, above-fold CTA density). Would have caught finding #2 much faster.
3. **`content-audit`** — scores content density, scannability, freshness (dates), redundancy. Recognitions + Press are *content* problems, not UI.
4. **`visual-regression`** — before/after Playwright screenshots at 3 viewports with diffs.
5. **`copy-audit-es`** — Spanish-language microcopy review (e.g., "Hacenos tu consulta" vs "Consultanos"), tone consistency.

### Missing agents
1. **`ux-auditor`** subagent — read-only, parallelizable. Takes URL + viewport list, runs Playwright, returns structured findings. Could be invoked from `/ship-check`.
2. **`a11y-auditor`** subagent — dedicated WCAG 2.2 pass with axe-core or pa11y instead of hand-rolled DOM sampling.
3. **`content-editor-es`** subagent — institutional Spanish reviewer for tone, redundancy, reading-level.

### Missing commands
- `/audit-site <url>` — spawns `ux-auditor` + `a11y-auditor` in parallel, returns merged report.
- `/audit-viewport <url> <breakpoint>` — targeted re-run after fixes.

### Architectural note
EPB `CONTEXT.md` lists audit skills as "Recommended" but no workflow forces an audit pass before the site is marked done. A `/ship-check` extension that runs a UX + a11y gate against a deployed URL would close the loop.
